Task 8 – VPN Setup and Privacy Report

##Objective
Understand the role of VPNs in protecting privacy and secure communication.

##Platform
Windows 10  
VPN Client: ProtonVPN Free

##Tools Used
ProtonVPN https://protonvpn.com
WhatIsMyIPAddress https://www.whatismyipaddress.com
Brave Browser

##Steps Performed

1. Signed up for a free ProtonVPN account.
2. Downloaded and installed the ProtonVPN client.
3. Connected to a VPN server (united stas and japan).
4. Verified IP address change using [whatismyipaddress.com].
5. Browsed secure websites (Wikipedia) to confirm encrypted traffic.
6. Disconnected VPN and compared browsing speed—minor delay during VPN use.


##VPN Benefits

- Encrypts internet traffic, protecting data from ISPs and public Wi-Fi snooping.
- Masks real IP address, enhancing anonymity.
- Bypasses geo-restrictions and censorship.
- Prevents tracking by advertisers and malicious actors.


## VPN Limitations

- Free VPNs may have limited speed or server options.
- VPN does not protect against phishing or malware directly.
- Some websites may block VPN traffic.
- Trust in VPN provider is essential—avoid shady services.
- Limited browsing speed.

##Browsing Speed Observation

- Before VPN: Pages loaded instantly, no delay.
- During VPN: Slight delay (~1–2 seconds) on media-heavy sites.
- After VPN: Speed returned to normal.

Conclusion: VPN added minor latency, but browsing remained smooth and secure.
